our story

On a rainy autumn day in 2008 at Cornell University, Nikki Nussbaum had forgotten her umbrella. She walked back from her a cappella practice to her apartment in Collegetown utterly drenched. As rainwater dripping from her hair and clothes formed a puddle on the elevator floor, she desperately tapped the door-close button, hoping no one would see her in this state. Alas, the doors failed to close fast enough, and onto the elevator stepped a tall, handsome, and perfectly dry David Sharon. He smiled in amusement as Nikki blushed, surprised to learn that this smooth character was none other than her next-door neighbor.

the venue

The Church

Many metro stations are located a short walking distance from the 3rd Quarter's Town Hall. Unless your hotel is walking distance from the site, you'll probably need to take the metro to get here. We advise you to familiarize yourself with the best route before travelling to Paris so you will have a clear idea of the best way to get there. If you need advice, don't hesitate to contact us. We'll be waiting for you outside of the building so, please try to be there on time (10am).

  • Venue
    3rd Quarter's Town Hall (Mairie du 3e arrondissement)
  • Address
    2 Rue Eugène Spuller, 3rd Quarter
  • Meeting Time
    The Ceremony starts exactly at 10:30, so we'll meet you all at 10am outside of the building.
The Reception

The Art gallery is located next to famous Sacré-Cœur church at Montmartre neighborhood. There are two main ways to get there. The first is to take the metro and get off at Abbesses station. From there, the reception is around a 12 minute walk. However, we should inform you that this is a very hilly area and walking there from the metro station requires climbing quite a few flights of stairs and routes. We strongly recommend you take a taxi or Uber o take you there (go to "Getting around" option from the homepage to learn a bit about how taxis and Uber operate in Paris).

  • Venue
    Art Gallery "Untitled Factory"
  • Address
    32 Rue Gabrielle, 18th Quarter
  • Meeting Time
    6pm
